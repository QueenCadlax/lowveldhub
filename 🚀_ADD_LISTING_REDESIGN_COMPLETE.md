## 📧 Email Application Flow

**User Journey:**
1. User sees hero + CTAs
2. Clicks "👉 Apply via Email"
3. Email client opens with:
   - **To:** info@lowveldhub.co.za
   - **Subject:** Application to Join LowveldHub
4. User copies example template or types details
5. User sends email with attachments (images, docs)
6. You receive email in inbox
7. Your team reviews manually
8. You respond with approval/feedback
9. Business gets featured

**Timeline:** 24–72 hours to approval
